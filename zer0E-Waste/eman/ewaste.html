E-Waste Handling Guide

Safety Procedures

🛡️ Personal Protection

Always wear gloves and safety glasses when handling e-waste. Use masks when dealing with dust.

⚠️ Hazardous Materials

Identify items with mercury, lead, or lithium batteries. Handle with extra care.

Collection Best Practices

📦 Packaging

Use separate containers for different e-waste categories. Prevent glass breakage.

🔋 Battery Handling

Tape battery terminals. Never stack loose batteries. Store in non-conductive containers.
